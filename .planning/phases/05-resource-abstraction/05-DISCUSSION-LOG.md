## CRUD LiveView Pages

### Architectural approach (discussion)

FilamentPHP uses separate page classes (ListRecords, CreateRecord, EditRecord) because PHP has no `live_action` equivalent. In Phoenix/LiveView, the idiomatic pattern is a single LiveView with `live_action` — as generated by `phx.gen.live`. The decision was made to follow the Phoenix pattern, not the FilamentPHP pattern.
